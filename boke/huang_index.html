【Title: Protecting Our Environment as a Junior High Student Nowadays, the environment we live in is facing serious problems like pollution and global warming. As a junior high school student, I realize that it's our duty to protect our environment. We should take actions from now on and do our best to make a difference. Firstly, we can start with simple things like not littering. Everyday, I see some of my classmates throw their trash on the ground carelessly. This is really bad for our school yard and the Earth. So, I always remind them to put their trash in the bins. By doing this, we can keep our surroundings clean and tidy. Secondly, saving water is also very important. Water is a precious resource that we cannot live without. Sometimes, I notice that some taps in our school are left running after use. I always make sure to turn them off tightly. At home, I also try to take shorter showers and use water wisely. Moreover, recycling is another way to help the environment. There are many things that can be recycled, such as paper, plastic bottles, and glass. I always separate my waste and put recyclable items in the recycling bins. By recycling, we can reduce the amount of waste that goes to landfills and save natural resources. However, sometimes I forget to bring my own shopping bags when I go shopping with my mom. This means we have to use plastic bags, which are harmful to the environment. I know I need to improve in this area and start remembering to bring my reusable bags every time. Lastly, I think we should all learn more about environmental issues and spread awareness among our friends and family. By educating others, we can make more people understand the importance of protecting our planet. In conclusion, protecting the environment is everyone's responsibility. As junior high school students, we can make a big impact by doing small things every day. Let's work together and make our world a better place to live in.
